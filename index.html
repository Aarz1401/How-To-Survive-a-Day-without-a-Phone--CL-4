About the Film

This interactive film explores different ways to survive a day without a phone. It follows the Story of a boy named Tom who wakes up to find his phone missing and must face the ultimate challenge of surviving a day without it. Throughout the day, he navigates the chaos of missing his phone , from awkwardly interacting with friends to discovering alternative sources of entertainment like sketching and appreciating nature. Despite initial panic and loneliness, Tom learns to embrace creative conversations, reconnect with the world around him, and enjoy genuine human connections. By the end of the day, he reflects positively on his experience, realising that life without a phone can be fulfilling and enjoyable.

At various stages, You get an option to choose Tom's path , choose your path wisely !!
